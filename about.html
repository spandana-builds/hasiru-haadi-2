Our Story

Hasiru Haadi means "The Green Path" — a journey rooted in love for nature, handmade creations, and mindful living. 🌱

We're two sisters from Bengaluru who started this small initiative to bring a little more green and a little more goodness into the world. Our path began with simple home-grown products—live plants, seeds, and natural soaps—all kind to the earth and crafted with intention.

Every item is handpicked or handmade with care, packed with heart, and delivered with gratitude. Whether you're a seasoned plant lover, a nature enthusiast, or simply curious about sustainable living, we welcome you. You're walking the green path with us.

Our Beliefs

  • Nature is our home — we cherish and protect it.
  • Simplicity is beauty — we keep our designs and products minimal.
  • Community is strength — we believe in supporting local and growing together.
